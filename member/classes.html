OUR CLASSES

Body Building

Want to tone, get muscle definition and feel sronger in your everyday life? Then our body building classes are made for you. You'll be walked through a set of exercises together with our top coaches at the start of tthe class with different rotations and number of repetitions as the weeks go by. Pair this with our HIIT cardio classes for maximum effect.

Boxing

Sometimes nothing feels better than putting on your gloves and hitting the crap out of a boxing bag or a set of pads. In our cardio boxing classes, we'll focus on great techniques to build stamina through boxing-inspired routines. Contact exercises are optional, meaning that you can get the benefits of a boxing workout without the fear of breaking someone's nose.

Yoga

From body building to powerlifting and everything in between, AFR Fitness Gym has the equipment and professional trainers to help push you to your limits and reach yor goals. With dumbells up to 150lbs, 10 squat racks, 15 deadlift platforms, 10 competition benches and a huge variety of plate loaded and pin loaded machines, we have everything you need.

HIIT Training

A solid high intensity cardio workout is the backbone of an effective workout regime, which is why we run HIIT classes twice daily. Whether you're right at the start of your weight loss journey or simply looking to tone up and keep fit, our classes and trainers will put you through your paces and keep you in top form. Both personal and group classes are available.
