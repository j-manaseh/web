GYM AMMENITIES

Weight Training

From body building to powerlifting and everything in between, AFR Fitness Gym has the equipment and professional trainers to help push you to your limits and reach yor goals. With dumbells up to 150lbs, 10 squat racks, 15 deadlift platforms, 10 competition benches and a huge variety of plate loaded and pin loaded machines, we have everything you need.

General Fitness

If your goals are to improve your overall health and wellness, we have everything you need. We have cardio equipment spanning two floors including 8 stepmills, 15+ treadmills, row machines, exercise bikes, spin bikes, ellipticals and arc trainers. In addition to cardio equipment, we have sretching areas with mats, foam rollers, bands, light weights and more.

Functional Training

With 60 feet of turf with a sled, stackable plyometric boxes, weighted medicine balls, battle ropes, squat racks and deadlift platforms, our functional training room has all the equipment you need to improve your athletic performance, boost muscle strength and endurance all to take your training to the next level.

Recovery Center

We have a fully fitted wellness center to help with post workout recovery, workout injuries, healthy pre workout routines and general relaxation. We have some of the best recovery technologies in the world under one roof: from cryotherapy, infrared saunas, compresiion treatment, hydromassage, float tanks, foam rolling and lymphatic drainage, we have everything you need to improve your athletic performance.

Personal Trainers

We have a large number of professional trainers with years of experience in the health and fitness industry who will help you create a healthy workout routine to quickly attain your goals. They offer both personal and group classes and are flexible to your workout schedule. whether it's yoga, boxing, srength training, body building, HIIT training or just cardio, we've got you!

Supplements and Shake Bar

Located conveniently within the gym, stop by our shake bar for pre and post workout necessities including protein shakes made to order, protein bars, pre workout drinks and powders, energy drinks, water, BCAAs, EAAs and other snacks as well as a wide variety of supplements to take home.
